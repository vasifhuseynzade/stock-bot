# V2.5 Strategy Synthesis Report

## Evidence summary

- v2.1b breakout-only had the best raw low-cost return, but was extremely cost-sensitive and weak in 2022.
- v2.4 primary VCP no-weak was the most robust across 25-50 bps and had the smallest drawdown, but generated too few trades.
- Pullback/reclaim and broad high-beta variants did not earn a place in the next core strategy.
- STRONG bucket trades carried most positive expectancy; MEDIUM names were the consistent drag unless filtered as exceptional cases.
- Trade-level filters that improved accepted breakout trades were: score >= 172/178, volume ratio >= 1.35, and stronger bucket/leader focus.

## V2.5 design decision

The new engine tests a synthesis: VCP robustness + leader breakout opportunity + major-index trend filters + no WEAK names + exceptional-only MEDIUM gating. The goal is not maximum historical return; it is a candidate that still works after 25-50 bps stress and does not get destroyed in 2022-like regimes.
